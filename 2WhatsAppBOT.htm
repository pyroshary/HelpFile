Dear Customer, you can now perform financial transaction which includes (Bank to Bank Transfer, Bank to Other Bank Transfer, Bill Payments, Credit Card payments “BAFL Credit Card and Other Bank Credit Cards only if added as beneficiary on Alfa app”.

Financial Transaction via WhatsApp BOT:
-Bank to Bank Transfer
-Bank to Other Bank Transfer
-Bill Payments
a.Mobile Payments
b.Credit Card payments (BAFL Credit Card and Other Bank Credit Cards only if added as beneficiary).
c.Utility bill Payments

Note: Financial Transactions is located in option number “7 – Transact” on main menu select 2 for self-service menu then of WhatsApp BOT.
Hi ▶ 7 – Transact ▶ Enter OTP ▶ Choose the desired options ▶ 1 – Bank to Bank Transfer 2- Bank to other Bank transfer 3 – Bill payments.

Dear Customer, utility bill payment process via WhatsApp BOT is mentioned below.

- Access Bank Alfalah’s WhatsApp BOT.
- From main menu, select option 7 for financial transactions.
- Enter the OTP, which you will receive on your registered mobile number.
- Select Option 3 “UTILITY BILL PAYMENT”.
- Enter the last four digits of your registered CNIC number.
- Select desired account by selecting its option number.
- Enter your year of birth as per CNIC.
- Type an option number of your desired beneficiary.
- Enter last four digits of you debit card number.
- Type “Pay” in order to pay your bill successfully.

Muaziz Saarif, WhatsApp BOT ke zariye diye gae utility bill payment process ko follow kar sakte hen.

- Bank Alfalah ka WhatsApp BOT access karen (WhatsApp chat channel ka number he 021 111 225 111).
- Main menu men option 7 select karen financial transactions ke liye.
- OTP enter karna hoga jo apke registered mobile number pe receive hoga.
- Option 3 "UTILITY BILL PAYMENTS" select karenge.
- CNIC number ke last four digits enter karne honge.
- Jis account ke zariye transaction karna chah rahe hen wo account select karna hoga, account ka option number select karke.
- CNIC ke mutabiq apna year of birth enter karen. For Example:1995
- Phir apko beneficiary select karna hoga jo ke ap us beneficiary ke option number se select kar sakte hen.
- Apne debit card number ke last four digits enter karne honge.
- Successfull payment ke liye "Pay" type karke send karna hoga.
