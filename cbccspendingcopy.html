◼ The customer must complete one of the two spending criteria for annual fee reversal.
◼ The customer may apply for reversal on an annual basis after annual fee is charged.
◼ The promotional period for spending starts after annual fee is charged to the account.
◼ Spending may be performed on either Basic or Supplementary card. 
◼ FED will not be reversed.
◼ Only Retail Spend and Cash Advance will be considered as eligible spend. 
◼ UBP, Alfa, QR transactions, Alfa Mall transactions, SBS, OSBS, COP, BTF are not included in spending.
◼ Transactions with UBP under MCC '0' should be excluded from the spending of Credit card annual fee reversal criteria.
◼ Only Retail Spend (POS & Ecommerce) and Cash Advance will be considered as eligible spend.
◼ If Annual fee charged till 31 Dec,2023 than old spending criteria 2023, will be followed
◼ For customers who are being charged with an old annual fee, they will have to follow the previous spending criteria. 
◼ If a customer performs an e-commerce transaction for bill payment at the merchant’s website through their payment gateway, it will count for reversal
◼ If a customer spends on Daraz or on any retail spend (POS/E-com) which later converts the amount in to SBS, accommodate such customers for annual fee reversals.
◼ For annual fee reversal, Spending is cumulative and could be done on any card, either spend on Basic card or Supplementary card.
◼ Customer have to complete the spending in 60 days and should inform the bank in total period of 90 days starting from the date of annual fee is charged. 
◼ If transaction is in pipeline before 60-day period but posted after this period, it may be considered as total spend for Annual fee reversal.
