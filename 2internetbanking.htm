Dear Customer, to subscribe to Bank Alfalah's internet banking services, follow these steps:

• Visit the internet banking registration page at https://netbanking.bankalfalah.com/ib/TermsConditions.aspx .
• Review the terms and conditions and select your desired option from Credit Card Customers, Debit Card Customers, or Account Holder Customers.
• Complete the required information, including account number or card number, CNIC/SCNIC/NICOP, date of birth, registered mobile number, and security question.
• Submit the form and confirm the registered email ID.
• Visit the ATM/CDM machine to or Bank Alfalah branch for biometric verification.
• Upon successful verification, an OTAC will be generated and sent to your registered email and mobile number.
• Use the OTAC to log in to internet banking.

Dear Customer, please follow the below steps to register on Alfa application.

1-Download the Alfa app from the Play store or App store through the links:
For Android Users https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.base.bankalfalah&hl=en
For iPhone Users https://itunes.apple.com/pk/app/alfa/id1050684372?mt=8
2-Click on Sign Up.
3-Click on Register with Alfa.
4-Enter CNIC/Passport Number (Then press Get started button).
5-Verify details via OTP Code (Allow auto-fetch to read and input OTP).
6-Set up your login credentials.
7-Validate username and password strength (Then press Continue button).
8-Then you will see screen of "Credentials set successfully".
9-Complete biometric requirements.
10-Set your login PIN after successfull biometric verification.
11-You will have an option feature of "Enable Touch/Face ID" after setting Login PIN.
12-Click on Finish Up button.
13-Your all existing relationships will appear.
14-Choose relationships to register with Alfa.
15-Mark default account for Alfa.
16-Choose daily transaction limit and accept terms and conditions then press Continue button.
17-Successfully register on Alfa.
18-You will be able to access Alfa app after a two hours of cool off period.

Please note: In-app biometric verification will let you use the app and conduct transactions but only with a limit of PKR 250,000 per day.
Branch biometric verification will allow the user to have the full Alfa experience on per day limit (selected package limit).

Debit Card Activation (English):
Dear Customer, you can activate your Debit Card through Alfa application, please follow the steps below:

3-Tap on ‘Go to card management’.

7-Again enter your card PIN for confirmation.
