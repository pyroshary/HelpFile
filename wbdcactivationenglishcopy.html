Dear Customer, you can activate your Debit Card by using our WhatsApp automated service, Alfa app, Internet banking, Alfalah Rapid and by calling at our helpline 021/042-111-225-111.

Debit Card Activation via WhatsApp Bot:
Step by step guide to activate your Debit Card via WhatsApp Bot Service:
• Access Bank Alfalah WhatsApp BOT.
• From main menu, select option 10 for Debit Card Activation.
• Enter last 4 digits of your Account Number e.g. 2387 .
• Enter last 4 digits of CNIC Number e.g. 7859 .
• Enter the year of birth e.g. 1986 (YYYY) .
• Enter last 4 digits of your Debit Card e.g. 2387 .
• Then you need to enter the OTP which you will receive on your registered mobile number (OTP will get expire in 10 minutes).
• You need to provide 4 digits Debit Card PIN Code .
• Re-Enter the same 4 digits of the Debit Card PIN Code .
• Then BOT will provide the confirmation message for the Activation of Debit Card, also you will be intimated via SMS.
Note: Chat needs to be initiated from your registered number for the activation of debit card via WhatsApp Bot services.

Debit Card Activation via Alfa App:
Dear Customer, you can activate your Debit Card through Alfa application, please follow the steps below:
• Login Alfa Application.
• Tap on "Overview".
• Tap on ‘Go to card management’.
• Tap on ‘Activate card now’.
• Enter card expiry (MM/YY).
• On next screen enter your card PIN.
• Again enter your card PIN for confirmation.
• On next screen Verify OTP (Tap on Allow button to Auto fetch OTP).
Next screen will come with message "Card activated & PIN set successfully!".

Debit Card Activation via Internet Banking:
To activate your Debit Card through Internet Banking, please follow the steps below:
• Access your Internet Banking by entering your login credentials.
• Navigate to the "Services" tab.
• Select "Card Management" from the available options.
• Choose the Card type then select Debit Card.
• Set a secure PIN for your Debit card.
• Click on the "Activation" button.
• Enter the One-Time Password (OTP) that was sent to your registered mobile number and email address.

Debit Card Activation via ATM:
You can activate your debit card by visiting any Bank Alfalah ATM with your Alfalah Debit Card, CNIC number and your registered mobile number.
• Insert your Alfalah Debit Card into the Bank Alfalah ATM Machine.
• Click ‘OK’ when the ATM machine ask you to activate your Debit Card.
• Enter your 13-digit CNIC number.
• Enter your date of birth (DD-MM-YYYY).
• You will receive a 4-digit OTP on your registered mobile number, enter the OTP and proceed.
• Enter a 4-digit ATM PIN of your choice using the keypad.
• Re-enter the ATM PIN for validation.
• You will receive the confirmation message for the Activation of Debit Card, also you will be intimated via SMS.
