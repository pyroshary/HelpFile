Dear Customer, you can check your Credit Card mini statement by using our WhatsApp automated service, Alfa app, Internet banking and by calling at our helpline 021/042-111-225-111.

Step by step guide to check your Credit Card mini statement via WhatsApp Bot Service:
• Access Bank Alfalah’s WhatsApp BOT.
• From main menu select 2 for self-service menu then, select option 1 for Account and Credit Card Services .
• Then Select Option 1 “Cards”.
• Then Select Option 2 “Credit Cards”.
• Then Select Option 3 “Credit Card Statement”.
• Then you need to enter the OTP which you will receive on your registered mobile number.
• Then you have to select credit card by typing its option number.
• Your credit card mini statement will reflect accordingly.

Dear Customer, you can download last 12 months credit card statement via WhatsApp BOT, Alfa App , Rapid or by calling our 24/7 service helpline 021/042 111 225 111. 

Process to download statement through WhatsApp BOT:
1-Select 2 for self-service menu then Press "1" for Account and Credit Card Services .
2-Press "5" for Download statement.
3-Enter OTP code which will send on your register mobile number.
4-Select your desire card by pressing its option number i.e 1,2,3
5-Select your desire month by pressing its option number i.e 1,2,3
6-Press "Y" to confirm your register email address.
7-Your credit card statement will be sent to your registered email address within 15 minutes. 

Process to download statement through Alfa app:
1-Login Alfa App.
2-Click on Overview option.
3-Click on "Go to card management" option on your Card.
4-Click on see statement.
5-Click on Get credit card statement via Email.

You will receive password protected E-Statement on email (9th, 10th, 11th and 12th digits of your credit card
will be used as password).
