Dear Customer, you can check your Account mini statement by using our WhatsApp automated service, Alfa app, Internet banking and by calling at our helpline 021/042-111-225-111.

Step by step guide to check mini Statement via WhatsApp Bot Service:
• Access Bank Alfalah’s WhatsApp BOT.
• From main menu, select option 1 for Account and Credit Card Services .
• Then Select Option 3 “Account balance Enquiry”.
• Then you need to enter the OTP which you will receive on your registered mobile number.
• Then you need to select the Account.
• Then Select Option 2 “Mini Statement”.
Your account mini statement will reflect accordingly.

In order to check and download your account statement through Alfa app please follow the below steps:
• Login Alfa app.
• Click on Overview button.
• Click on see statement.
• Click on Get account statement via Email.
Then you will be able to get your account statement through email.
