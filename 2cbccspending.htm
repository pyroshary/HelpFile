 Important points 
- The reversal criteria will be applicable on all fee based existing and new customers.
- Both Basic and Supplementary cardholders must complete their independent criteria to qualify
for annual fee reversal.
- The promotional period for spending starts after annual fee is charged to the Credit Card.
- Annual fee reversals will be automatically processed within 2–3 working days after the completion of the required spending. Once the reversal is posted, customers will be notified via SMS.
- FED will not be subject to reversal under this policy.
- Bank Alfalah Visa Ultra Cashback Cardholders are only eligible for 1st Year Annual Fee Waivers
upon spending PKR 75,000 within 2 months.
- Bank Alfalah American Express Cardholders are not eligible for reversal under these criteria.
- Only Retail Spend (POS & E-Commerce) and Cash Advance will be considered as eligible spend.
- Utility Bill Payment via Alfa, Alfa Mall transactions, QR, SBS, OSBS, COP, BTF do not qualify as retail
spend transactions.

In Case if the Annual fee is not reversed in 2 to 3 working days after spending, then you have to call our helpline 021 111 225 111 for annual fee reversal request.
