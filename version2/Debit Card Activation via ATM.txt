Debit Card Activation via ATM:
You can activate your debit card by visiting any Bank Alfalah ATM with your Alfalah Debit Card, CNIC number and your registered mobile number.
• Insert your Alfalah Debit Card into the Bank Alfalah ATM Machine.
• Click ‘OK’ when the ATM machine ask you to activate your Debit Card.
• Enter your 13-digit CNIC number.
• Enter your date of birth (DD-MM-YYYY).
• You will receive a 4-digit OTP on your registered mobile number, enter the OTP and proceed.
• Enter a 4-digit ATM PIN of your choice using the keypad.
• Re-enter the ATM PIN for validation.
• You will receive the confirmation message for the Activation of Debit Card, also you will be intimated via SMS.

Debit Card Activation via ATM:
Ap apna debit card activate kar sakte hen by visiting any Bank Alfalah ATM. Alfalah Debit Card, CNIC number aur registered mobile number ke sath ATM visit karna hoga.
• Alfalah Debit Card insert karen Bank Alfalah ATM Machine men.
• Click ‘OK’ when the ATM machine ask you to activate your Debit Card.
• Apna 13-digits ka CNIC number enter karen.
• Apni date of birth enter karen (DD-MM-YYYY).
• Apke pas registered mobile number pe OTP receive hoga. OTP enter karen ATM men phir proceed karden.
• Phir ap apni marzi ka PIN code type karen.
• Phir ap wohi PIN code dubara enter karen for validation.
• Phir apko confirmation message receive hoga Debit Card activation ka, aur SMS ke zariye bhi intimate kiya jaega.
