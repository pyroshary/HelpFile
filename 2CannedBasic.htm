CDM CASH DEPOSIT PROCESS:
Cash Deposit into Bank Alfalah Account:
1. Select Deposit to Alfalah� from the main screen.
2. Enter CNIC and provide thumb impression for biometric verification.
3. Enter Bank Alfalah Account number and Mobile number.
4. Validate account details.
5. Deposit cash into the machine and verify cash count on screen and press deposit.
6. Collect receipt on successful transaction.
Cash Deposit into Other Bank Account:
1. Select Deposit to Alfalah’ from the main screen.
2. Enter CNIC and provide thumb impression for biometric verification.
3. Select desired bank from the given list.
4. Enter Bank Alfalah Account number and Mobile number.
5. Validate account details.
6. Deposit cash into the machine and verify cash count on screen and press deposit.
7. Collect receipt on successful transaction.
